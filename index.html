What you will get from our courses

Here are everything you will get from us after completing the course

Course Certificate

Everyone will get a course completion certificate which can be scanned and verified from our website. You can download the soft copy of the certificate online and the printed copy will be delivered to your address.

Opportunity to work with us

Among those who will do well in the course, 3 people will be screened and given the opportunity of paid internship in our company. Also, you will get the opportunity to work directly with Sorowar Mahabub, the CEO & founder of চলো, Programming শিখি .

Lifetime course access

Those who purchase the course once, will receive lifetime access to all course content and will continue to receive all future updates. In addition, you will have lifetime access to the private group where we will try to answer your questions.

24/7 Support Group

For this course we have created a private Whatsapp group where 4-5 other male/female Teaching Assistant (TA) along with course instructor Sorowar Mahabub will directly answer your various questions. We promise to answer your questions within 24 hours maximum. But in most cases you will get the answer in less time than that. Besides, every week there will be live session on the discussed module where you can directly ask questions and get answers.
